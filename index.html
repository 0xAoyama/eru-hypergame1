How to Play
🎮
Move or Watch
WASD or Arrow keys to move. Touch drag on mobile. Leave idle and AI takes over.
⚔️
Auto-Attack
Weapons fire automatically at nearby enemies. Survive and collect XP gems to level up.
⬆️
Level Up
Pick 1 of 3 upgrades: new weapons or passive boosts. Build your loadout wisely!
🌊
Waves
Enemy waves get stronger over time. Boss appears every 5 waves. How long can you survive?
Weapons
🔮
Orbit
Spinning orbs deal contact damage around you. More orbs & bigger radius as level goes up.
✨
Magic Bolt
Auto-fires homing projectiles at nearest enemy. Gains pierce and multi-shot at higher levels.
⚡
Lightning
Strikes a random enemy in range. Chains to more targets as level increases.
🌟
Holy Aura
Continuous damage to all enemies in a small radius. Consistent DPS, great for crowds.
💥
Shockwave
Periodic explosion with knockback. Huge burst damage on a cooldown.
